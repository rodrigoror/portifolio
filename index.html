Who Am I ?

About Me

With over two decades immersed in software development, I've cultivated a journey from foundational IT support to becoming a Tech Manager and Solutions Architect. My career is defined by a passion for building robust, scalable systems and fostering high-performing teams. I specialize in designing cloud-native solutions, leveraging technologies like Node.js, TypeScript, and MongoDB, and driving innovation through AI-assisted development.
My experience spans diverse domains, from optimizing JVM performance and leading mobile app development to pioneering B2B SaaS solutions and integrating complex financial data. As an entrepreneurial leader, I thrive on connecting technical excellence with business strategy, always focused on delivering impactful products from conception to successful deployment and beyond.

What I Do ?

Service
Mentorship & AI-Driven Productivity

I foster high-performing teams, mentoring developers and leads in agile practices. By embedding AI tools like Cursor and LLM Agents, I've driven an 80% improvement in developer productivity and accelerated project delivery.

Fullstack Development & Microservices

Expert in crafting robust fullstack solutions across mobile (Flutter, Android) and web (Vue.js, Java frameworks, Coldfusion, jQuery). I design and implement scalable microservices using Node.js, TypeScript, MongoDB, and GraphQL, ensuring seamless API integrations and optimal performance.

DevSecOps, Quality & Observability

Boosting code quality and CI/CD with DevSecOps and TDD, implementing SonarQube for 80% code coverage. I ensure end-to-end observability using tools like OpenTracing, Prometheus, and Grafana for robust site reliability.

Data-Driven Solutions & Architecture

Experienced in designing and implementing business intelligence dashboards, managing high-volume data (400k+ daily records in MongoDB), and applying Domain-Driven Design (DDD) to align software architecture with strategic business domains for impactful financial and analytics tools.
